Franklin's Virtues — PWA package
=================================

Files:
- index.html        (main app)
- manifest.json     (PWA manifest)
- service-worker.js (offline caching)
- franklin_logo.png (placeholder 1024x1024 icon)

How to deploy on Netlify (drag & drop):
1. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag and drop the contents of this zip (index.html, manifest.json, service-worker.js, franklin_logo.png)
3. Wait for deployment and visit the generated HTTPS URL.
4. On iPhone (Safari): open the URL, tap 'Share' → 'Add to Home Screen'.

Notes:
- Replace franklin_logo.png with your preferred high-quality icon if desired.
- The app includes Export/Import (Backup) buttons in the top-right to save and restore your data as JSON.
